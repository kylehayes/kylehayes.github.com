Intel Mac Folks: VMWare Fusion for $40

Sep 19th, 2007 | Comments

Hey all you happy Intel Mac folk, I highly recommend if you are currently using Parallels or if you need a virtual machine solution for your Mac to run Windows and other operating systems on top of your Mac to check out VMWare Fusion. I am don’t know what the technical differences are from Parallels, but what I do know is that just using it, I like the way it performs a lot better.

Right now is a great time to buy, I was browsing Amazon and noticed that it was at a really great price. It normally retails at about $80, but Amazon’s price is about $60. Amazon also has a $20 rebate on the product right now, bringing it down to just over $40.

Check it out at Amazon!
